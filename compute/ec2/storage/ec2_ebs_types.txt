- General purpose SSD:
  - Used for dev/test environments and smaller DB instances
  - Up to 10.000 IOPS per volume
  - From 1GiB to 16TiB
- Provisioned IOPS SSD
  - Used for mission critical applications that require sustained IOPS performance
  - Large database workloads
  - From 4GiB to 16TiB
  - Up to 20.000 IOPS per volume
- Magnetic:
  - Low storage cost
  - Used for workload when performance is not important or data is infrequently accessed
  - From  500GiB to 16TiB
